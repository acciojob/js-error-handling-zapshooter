Expression Evaluator

Evaluates integer arithmetic with + - * /. Invalid characters throw OutOfRangeError; malformed expressions throw InvalidExprError.

Allowed: integers, spaces, + - * /. Anything else is out of range. Press Enter or click Evaluate.

Try an example

10 + 5 * 2 1 + -2 100 / 4 - 3 a + 2 ❌ 1+/2 ❌ +1 + 2 ❌ 1+2+ ❌
